Services FAQ

Answers to some of the questions we get most often about our services and process.

What types of projects do you take on?

We work across hospitality, residential, and commercial projects—from boutique spaces to full-scale developments.

Do you offer design-only services?

Yes. We can support you with design and documentation only, or manage the entire turnkey execution depending on your needs.

How early should we involve you in a project?

The earlier the better. Involving us at the planning or pre-lease stage often saves time, revisions, and cost later.

Do you work with clients in other cities or countries?

Yes. We collaborate remotely with clients across geographies and can travel for key milestones when required.

How do your fees work?

Fees depend on scope, complexity, and engagement model (design-only, supervision, or turnkey). We share a clear proposal after the discovery phase.
